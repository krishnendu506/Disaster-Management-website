Dr. Emily Carter (Expert)Fire Safety Advice

As a fire safety expert, I recommend installing smoke detectors on every level of your home and testing them monthly. During a fire, crawl low under smoke and use the back of your hand to check door handles for heat before opening.

Maria Gonzalez (Survivor)Flood Experience

In the 2017 Hurricane Harvey, our neighborhood was flooded quickly. We moved to the attic and used a mattress as a raft to reach higher ground. Never underestimate rising water — it can happen faster than you think.
